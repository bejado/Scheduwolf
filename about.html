About

Right now, signing up for classes is like trying to solve a thousand-piece puzzle with a blindfold on. With the average cost of a four-unit class at USC over $6,000, choosing the right schedule is a huge investment. Furthermore, with a schedule of four classes, there are over 3,000 different combinations in which they can be arranged. It is difficult and frustrating for students to build the schedule they want with so many possibilities, yet they trudge through this backwards process every semester. USC's registration system looks and feels like it was built in the 90's. With Scheduwolf, we are confident we can modernize not only the interface, but the entire registration experience.

Currently, Scheduwolf is still in Beta testing. Not all course data may be accurate. There is a few hours of delay between when USC's Schedule of Class API is updated and when the informaiton propogates to our site. Always check WebReg for the most accurate information.
